Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is MyNext?

MyNext is a 2-day event targeted for Malaysian students in North America that is aimed to produce students with business aspirations, students with deep knowledge enough to invest and be involved in real estate or equity markets so that when they return home, they are not only self-sufficient by themselves but they can help propel our economy as well.

We plan to invite successful speakers and entrepreneurs to share their knowledge and experiences to help develop skills and entrepreneurship interests in students.

Is this event for me?

If you are interested to understand how to do financial planning, learn the concept of time value of money, learn how to invest your money or even learn how to start a business, this event is essentially for you!

Who can join?

This event is open to anyone who have a deep interest in investment, global finance, business and entrepreneurship. Our main target is for Malaysian students currently studying in North America. We have a limit of 75 participants for this event, so please register right now in order to reserve your spot.

Who is coming?

We have a couple of prominent and successful speakers that have vast experiences in their respective fields. For more information, please go to http://www.mynext2015.com/schedule for a complete list of speakers and schedules of the event.

When is it going to happen?

This event will be held on May 2-3, 2015 (Saturday and Sunday) from 9AM to 5PM.

Where is it going to happen?

Address: 40 St George St, Toronto, ON M5S 2E4

Events

What kind of activities will be held?

Most of the slots will involve a 30 minutes forum style session, where there is a moderator and a speaker that will discuss a certain topic. Participants will have the chance to ask questions at the end of the session.

On top of that, we will have a group discussion slot, where participants will be divided in groups and will be assigned a certain topic to be discussed. Each group is required to present the outcomes of their discussion and there will be a proceeding questions and answers session.
